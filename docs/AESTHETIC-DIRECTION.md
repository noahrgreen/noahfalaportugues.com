# Aesthetic Direction Memo

## Direction
Composed editorial minimalism with subtle Brazilian tonal cues.

## Design Decisions
- Warm off-white background and paper-card surfaces for calm authority.
- Deep green accent used sparingly for CTA and navigational emphasis.
- Strong whitespace and restrained hierarchy.
- Serif display + clean sans body pairing for cultural seriousness and readability.

## What Was Avoided
- Tourist-flag visual clichés
- Template-heavy startup UI patterns
- Over-animated effects
- Overstated marketing visuals
