## Post 1 — Common Mistake
Intent:
Show one high-frequency English-speaker mistake with correction and practical explanation.

Caption:
A common mistake I still hear (and used to make) in Brazilian Portuguese:

"Eu sou 30 anos."

Natural correction:
"Eu tenho 30 anos."

In Portuguese, age is expressed with TER, not SER.
Small correction, big difference in sounding natural.
