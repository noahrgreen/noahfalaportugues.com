# Brand Direction Packet

## Option A — Editorial Authority (Selected)
- Tone: disciplined, composed, practical
- Typography: elegant serif headers + neutral sans body
- Palette: warm neutrals with restrained deep green accents
- Layout: clean blocks, high spacing, minimal visual noise
- Cultural cues: subtle Brazilian seriousness through language and context, not icon clichés

## Option B — Quiet Modern Utility
- Tone: direct, utility-focused
- Typography: sans-forward with lighter serif accents

- Layout: denser content framing
- Cultural cues: minimal and mostly textual

## Option C — Soft Cultural Premium
- Tone: refined, slightly warmer
- Typography: classic serif emphasis
- Palette: cream + dark forest + soft brass accent
- Layout: larger hero and fewer cards per row
- Cultural cues: mild Rio-inspired color warmth
