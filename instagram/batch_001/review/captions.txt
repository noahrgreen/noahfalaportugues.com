Most English speakers get this wrong in Portuguese.

You don’t “be” an age in Portuguese — you “have” it.

"Eu sou 30 anos" sounds literal from English.
"Eu tenho 30 anos" sounds natural in Brazil.

Small correction, immediate improvement in conversation.
