CAROUSEL 01 — COMMON MISTAKE
Hook: Most English speakers get this wrong in Portuguese.

You don’t “be” an age in Portuguese — you “have” it.

"Eu sou 30 anos" sounds literal from English.
"Eu tenho 30 anos" is the natural way in Brazil.

Small correction. Immediate difference.
