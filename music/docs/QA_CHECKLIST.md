# Music QA Checklist

Per reel, validate:
1. Voice is clear and always dominant.
2. Music remains subtle (target 10-20% against voice).
3. No clipping or harsh peaks.
4. Loop/trim transitions are smooth.
5. Track tone matches category and brand posture.
6. No vocals and no lyrics.
7. Copyright status remains internal-safe.

Fail conditions:
- Music competes with voice
- Sudden loud transitions
- Distracting rhythm for educational speech
